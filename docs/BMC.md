## Narrative Summary

**project‑boundary** is a SaaS/OSS hybrid that equips maintainers with AI‑assisted, versioned “project scope maps”. By ingesting issue, PR, and roadmap data (leveraging Axentx’s 18 M+ paired datasets) and running them through a high‑throughput inference stack (vLLM + SGLang), the tool flags work that lies outside the declared boundary, suggests boundary adjustments, and surfaces risk metrics.
